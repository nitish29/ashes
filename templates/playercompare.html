Rather than using a traditional metric like batting average, which can be inflated because of good performances in only few matches, we use Consistency Adjusted Average (CAA) as a new performance metric that measures the consistency in a player's performance over the course of the tournament.

Match Progress vs. Batting Impact Factor

Batting Impact Factor measures a playerâ€™s performance in the context of a given match. It not only takes into account total runs scored by a player but also the pace at which he scored his runs, his batting performance evaluated against other batsmen and conditions like strength of the opponent team, pressure situation, wickets that have fallen when the player comes into bat and other similar parameters.
